👪 For Grown-Ups

What this is: a little reading game made just for Theo. The words, stories, and phonics match his summer reading pack, one week at a time. It runs right in the browser — no accounts, no ads, and nothing leaves the device. It even works offline once opened.

The two modes

  • 🖼️ Picture Play — listen-and-tap picture games. Short 3-question rounds, read-aloud always on. Best when he's tired or just starting out.
  • 🔤 Word Explorer — sight words, decodable stories, word building, and writing. Slightly longer rounds for when he's ready to read.

He can use either one any day — his progress is shared between them.

Put it on his tablet or phone

  • iPad / iPhone: open this page in Safari, tap the Share button, then “Add to Home Screen.”
  • Android: open in Chrome, tap the ⋮ menu, then “Add to Home screen.”

It then opens full-screen like a real app and works without internet.

Buttons inside the game

  • 🔊 Read Aloud and 🎵 Sound FX — tap to turn on/off (bottom “My Blocks” row).
  • 📅 Week — jump to any week; it starts on the right week for today's date automatically.
  • 👪 Grown-Ups — a progress summary and a “reset progress” button.
  • ⬅︎ (top left) — go back.

How he earns

🟩 Emeralds for every right answer → a 💎 diamond for every 10 emeralds → collect 🐾 mobs as he goes. Rounds are short on purpose so he wins often.

Need something changed?

The content is maintained by Katy. To request an update — new words, a new game, or next year's curriculum — just message Katy with (1) which mode & week and (2) what to change. It's built from a simple word list, so updates are quick. You don't need to edit anything yourself.
